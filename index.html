Campus Life

School Gym

Lot of exercise equipment

The physical education teachers care about your health very much and they try and challenge you with various sports and exercises. The school has recently added a pickleball court for students to engage in the sport of pickleball.



School Library

Very large and interesting

The library is very large and has a lot of different types of books. There is also a 3D printer in the library where students can design and print their own 3D objects.



School Arts

Unique oppurtunities

There are various art oppurtunities at JTMS such as painting and pottery as well as photography and video production. There is also a lot of music oppurtunities for any singers or musicians.
